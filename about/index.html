Svitlana is a professional Ukrainian pianist, operage coach, music manager and teacher, whose talent knows no bounds

With 8 years of experience, Svitlana has mastered the art of piano performance, coaching opera singers and imparting a comprehensive music education

Ever since she was young, a flame for classical music was kindled in her heart, and that passion is continuing to burn brightly throughout her entire life

Svitlana has performed in chamber and concert halls of Italy, Czech Republic, Lithuania and Germany

She different international competitions and festivals: «Virtuosi per musica di pianoforte» Usti nad Labem in the Czech Republic, the award Carpathian fondation Development Culture, awarded the "Identity of the Forum" within the international forum Pianists «Bieszczady bez granic» in Sanok - Poland, winner of 3 prize of the contest «Giovani musicisti» in m.Trevizo - Italy.

To improve her musical skills she has attended master-class with the following outstanding musicians: Andrzej Jasinski, Isabella Lo Porto, Eugene Indjic, Vera Nosina, Mikhail Voskresensky, Alessandra Zhvirblitte, Andrzej Tatarski, Jarosław Drzewiecki, Lukas Geniušas, Michael Striharzh.
